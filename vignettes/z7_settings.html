7 - Save/load settings

Simon Garnier

This page applies to trackR("classic"), trackR("quanta"), and trackR("tracktor").

At any time during the parameterization of trackR, you can save your tracking parameters for later use.

In order to do this, simply click on the Save settings button at the bottom of the control panel. The settings will be saved at a location of your choice in the form of a .Rds file.

To restore saved settings, click on the Load settings button, navigate to the location of a previously saved .Rds setting file and select it. This should restore trackR to the state it was in when you first saved these settings.
